ABOUT NASABITS

Nasabits is a non-profit organization based in Nasarawa State, Nigeria, that offers young people technology training and career development opportunities. The mission of the organization is to provide young people with digital skills and opportunities to work in the technology industry while promoting inclusivity and diversity in the digital economy.

Nasabits provides an array of training courses in subjects like web development, software engineering, data analysis, and digital marketing.
