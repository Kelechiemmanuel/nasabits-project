ABOUT TECH FOR ALL

Nasabits is a non-profit organization based in Nasarawa State, Nigeria, that offers young people technology training and career development opportunities. The mission of the organization is to provide young people with digital skills and opportunities to work in the technology industry while promoting inclusivity and diversity in the digital economy.

TECH4ALL

Students Selection

We choose students based on their dedication and motivation and provide them with training in creative technologies, business, and life skills that is demand-aligned, guiding kids to chances for earning money.

Our local partners are vital for the implementation of the project. They provide a solid governance structure with management, advisory boards, selection of capable trainers in the ICT field, selection of the youth.

Their local network and partnerships with local CBO's are crucial for the selection of youth from underprivileged areas. Our alumni never stop learning and end up at the forefront of change.

NasaBits

NasaBits is a youth based social enterprise that uses ICT creatively to improve the lives of less privileged youth from non-formal settlement
